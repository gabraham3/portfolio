About Me

I'm a game developer who's contributed to Cash Out Club, a shipped mobile title, during internships. Outside of that, I build my own projects to keep exploring what interests me: Apotheosis Comics, a deckbuilder roguelike I built solo in Unity, a compiler for a subset of Kotlin I wrote with one other collaborator, and Throne, a war-game strategy title with D&D-style roleplaying that I'm solo-developing for my friends.

What I care about most is making games with mechanics that are fun to play with, systems that let players find cool, expressive ways to approach the game instead of just one right answer.
